Events

Welcome to Art Amateurs exciting lineup of upcoming events! Join us in celebrating the world of art through these engaging and enriching experiences. Don't miss out on these opportunities to connect with fellow art enthusiasts, explore new artistic horizons, and engage with the creative community.

Artistic Showcase: "Colors of Expression"

  • Date: Saturday, October 15, 2023
  • Time: 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM
  • Location: Art Amateurs Gallery, Amsterdam.
  • Description: Immerse yourself in a kaleidoscope of artistic expression at our "Colors of Expression" showcase. Featuring a diverse collection of paintings, sculptures, and multimedia art, this event is a testament to the boundless creativity of our local artists. Meet the artists, engage in discussions, and enjoy an evening of artistic wonderment.

Creative Workshop: "Brush and Beyond"

  • Date: Saturday, October 15, 2023
  • Time: 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM
  • Location: Art Amateurs Studio, Amsterdam.
  • Description: Unleash your inner artist in our "Brush and Beyond" creative workshop. Led by a renowned local artist, you'll learn various painting techniques, experiment with different mediums, and create your masterpiece. No prior experience is necessary; all materials will be provided. Come prepared to explore, learn, and have fun!

Art Talks: "Inspiring Perspectives"

  • Date: Wednesday, December 20, 2023
  • Time: 7:00 PM - 8:30 PM
  • Location: Art Amateurs Community Center, Amsterdam.
  • Description: Join us for an enlightening evening of "Inspiring Perspectives." Engage in thought-provoking discussions with established artists and art scholars as they share their insights, stories, and experiences in the art world. Whether you're an aspiring artist, an art collector, or simply curious about the world of art, this event promises to expand your horizons

Registration:
